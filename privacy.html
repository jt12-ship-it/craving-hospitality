Privacy Policy

Plain-language summary of how we handle the information you share with us.

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Craving Hospitality ("we", "us", "our") operates the website thisiscravings.com. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit our site or contact us, how we use it, and what your rights are. We keep things simple because we genuinely respect your privacy.

1. Who we are

Craving Hospitality is a fresh-food catering company based in Lebanon. You can reach us at:

  • Phone / WhatsApp: +961 71 590 222
  • Instagram: @cravinghospitalitylb
  • Website: thisiscravings.com

2. Information we collect

Contact & catering inquiry forms

Our forms ask for your name, phone number, email address (optional), preferred contact method, and your message or event details. When you submit a form, no data is sent to our servers. Instead, your browser opens WhatsApp with a pre-filled message containing the details you entered. The conversation then takes place entirely within WhatsApp, which is subject to WhatsApp's own privacy policy.

Menu bookmarks

If you use the ♥ "Add to Inquiry" feature on the Menu page, your selections are saved in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is only used to pre-fill your catering inquiry message. Clearing your browser data removes it.

Usage analytics

We may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with the site (pages visited, buttons clicked, time on page). Google Analytics uses cookies and may transfer data to Google's servers. You can opt out via the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on or by adjusting your browser's cookie settings.

Information you share directly

If you call or WhatsApp us directly, we receive only what you choose to share in that conversation. We do not record calls.

3. How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiry or plan your catering event.
  • To communicate with you about your order or event via your preferred channel.
  • To improve the website experience (analytics data only, in aggregate).

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-party services

This site connects to the following external services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • WhatsApp — used to receive and reply to inquiries.
  • Instagram — our social media profile (@cravinghospitalitylb). Visiting it is subject to Meta's policies.
  • Google Fonts — loads the Baloo 2 and Plus Jakarta Sans typefaces. Google may log the request IP.
  • Google Analytics — website usage analytics (if active).

5. Cookies

Our website itself does not set first-party cookies. Google Analytics may set cookies if it is active. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Data retention

Because our website does not store form submissions on a server, we retain no personally identifiable data on our end beyond what you voluntarily share in a WhatsApp or phone conversation. WhatsApp conversation history is subject to WhatsApp's own retention policies.

7. Your rights

You have the right to ask us what personal information we hold about you (received via direct communication), to request corrections, or to ask us to stop contacting you. To exercise any of these rights, reach us on WhatsApp or phone at +961 71 590 222.

8. Children's privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Chat with us on WhatsApp — it's the fastest way to reach us.
